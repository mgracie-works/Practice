Recipe for Kimchi


Kimchi

Ingredients

  • Napa Cabbage
  • Gochugaru
  • Radish
  • Carrots
  • Apple
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Fish Sauce
  • Potato
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Wash and chop the Napa Cabbage, carrots and radish.
  2. After chopping the Napa Cabbage into small pieces, cover the cabbage with salt and water and let it sit on the side for 1 hour.
  3. Boil the Potato until soft and put it on the side.
  4. Grate the remaining ingredients and combine them together to make the paste:
    1. Grate the Onions, garlic, apple, ginger to make a paste
    2. Mash the boiled potato and mix with the paste
    3. Add salt and fish sauce and then mix the Gochugaru on the mixed paste:
      • Note: Continue to add the fish sauce and gochugaru depending on your desired taste
  5. Once the paste is done, mix the paste with the chopped carrots and radish.
  6. Go back to the Napa cabbage and wash it until all the salt and the water is removed.
  7. Mixed the paste with the Napa Cabbage.
  8. Set it aside for 1 hour before putting it in the fridge to ferment.

Congrats on making your Kimchi!
